## Technical Field
Control mechanisms improving reliability and predictability of computer
systems executing AI-assisted tasks.

## Objective Technical Problem
How to maintain deterministic and verifiable system behavior in AI-assisted
enterprise execution environments.

## Technical Means
- Stage-aware arbitration of execution and generation
- Coordinate-bounded capability enforcement
- Deterministic governance load shedding
- Replayable control decisions

## Technical Effect
- Improved system stability
- Reduced risk of irreversible execution errors
- Deterministic and explainable control flow
- Enhanced auditability and compliance readiness

## No Business Logic
All decisions are system-state based and deterministic; no subjective or
business judgment is required.
